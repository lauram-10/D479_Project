Hotels and Resorts

  • Coral Bay Resort (Four Star Luxury)

    • Taniti's premier four-star resort located on the southern coast. Coral Bay Resort offers beachfront villas, fine dining, and infinity pool, spa services, and daily cultural shows. Perfect for luxury travelers seeking full-service relaxation.

  • Taniti Shores Hotel

    • A modern, mid-range hotel in the heart of Merriton Landing. Guests enjoy comfortable rooms, an onsite cafe, and easy walking access to shops, entertainment, and beaches.

  • Island Breeze Inn

    • A family-owned hotel with cozy rooms and friendly service. Located just outside Taniti City, it offers a quiet stay with beautiful garden views and complimentary breakfast.

  • Mango Tree Lodge

    • Nestled near the rainforest edge, this eco-friendly hotel combines comfort with adventure. It's ideal for hikers and nature lovers and offers guided tours and organic, locally-sourced meals.

  • The Seagrass Hotel

    • A stylish boutique hotel along Yellow Leaf Bay featuring locally made furnishings, rooftop lounge views, and bicycle rentals for guests. Perfect for couples or solo travelers.

Hostels and B&Bs

  • Tiki Trail Hostel (Budget-Friendly)

    • A popular stop for backpackers and solo travelers. Offers clean dorm-style rooms, shared kitchens, and group tours. Located just a 10 minute walk from the central bus station.

  • Seaside Stay B&B

    • A charming family-run bed & breakfast just steps from the beach. Guests rave about the homemade breakfast, warm hospitality, and oceanfront hammocks.

  • Hibiscus Haven B&B

    • Located in a quiet residential area of Taniti City, this colorful B&B offers three private rooms with en-suite baths. The hosts are long-time locals who love sharing Tanitian culture with the guests.

  • Rainforest Roost Hostel

    • An adventurous hostel located near the Rainforest Canopy Walkway. Offers bunk beds, guided hikes, and a common fire pit for evening gatherings.

  • Palm Garden Guesthouse

    • A peaceful bed and breakfast surrounded by tropical gardens. Features five private rooms and an open-air breakfast nook serving local fruit and baked goods.
