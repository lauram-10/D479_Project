Sightseeing

  • Mount Turoa Volcano Trail (Moderate)

    • Embark on a guided hike to the summit of Taniti's active volcano, Mount Turoa. The trail offers panoramic views of the island's diverse landscapes, from lush rainforests to coastal vistas.

  • Coral Bay Snorkeling Reef (Beginner to Advanced)

    • Explore the vibrant underwater world at Coral Bay, home to colorful coral formations and a variety of marine life. It's a favorite spot for both the novice and experienced snorkelers.

  • Rainforest Canopy Walkway (Moderate)

    • Experience the island's rich biodiversity from above on the Rainforest Canopy Walkway. This elevated path winds through the treetops, offering unique perspectives of the flora and fauna.

  • Taniti Cultural Heritage Center (All Ages)

    • Delve into the island's history and traditions at the Taniti Cultural Heritage Center. Interactive exhibits and performances provide insight into the indigenous Tanitian culture.

  • Sunset Point Lookout (All Ages)

    • Capture breathtaking view of the sunset over the Pacific Ocean from this popular vantage point. It's an ideal spot for photography and relaxation.

  • Lava Tube Caves Exploration (Moderate to Advanced)

    • Discover the island's geological wonders by exploring the network of lava tube caves formed by ancient volcanic activity. Guided tours are available for safe exploration.

  • Taniti Botanical Gardens (All Ages)

    • Stroll through the beautifully maintained Taniti Botanical Gardens, showcasing a diverse collection of native and exotic plant species. It's a tranquil retreat for nature enthusiasts.

  • Whale Watching Experience (Suitable for Those 10 and Over)

    • Seasonal whale watching tours offer the chance to observe majestic humpback whales during their migration. Knowledgeable guides provide educational insights during the excursions.

  • Island Artisans Market (All Ages)

    • Browse handcrafted goods, including traditional textiles, jewelry, and carvings, at the Island Artisans Market. It's a great place to find unique souvenirs and support local artisans.

  • Taniti Lighthouse (All Ages)

    • Visit the historic Taniti Lighthouse, which has guided ships safely to harbor for over a century. The site offers panoramic views and a glipse into the island's maritime history.

Entertainment

  • Rainforest Zipline Adventure (Family Friendly)

    • Soar through the jungle canopy for an unforgettable bird's eye view of Taniti's lush rainforest. Perfect for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike.

  • Merriton Landing Dance Club

    • Experience Taniti's nightlife at the island's newest dance venue, featuring live DJs, themed nights, and tropical cocktails.

  • Taniti History Museum (Family Friendly)

    • Discover the island's rich cultural heritage through exhibits on indigenous traditions, colonial history, and volcanic activity.

  • Coral Bay Snorkeling Tours (Family Friendly)

    • Join guided snorkeling trips to vibrant reefs just off the coast, where you can spot sea turtles, colorful fish, and coral gardens.

  • Chartered Deep Sea Fishing Tours (Family Friendly - ages 10+ recommended)

    • Embark on half- or full-day fishing excursions with local guides. Ideal for beginners and seasoned anglers.

  • Microbrewery at Merriton Langing

    • Taste unique island-brewed beers made with tropical ingredients and enjoy a relaxed atmosphere with pub food and live music.

  • Island Cinemas (Family Friendly)

    • Catch the latest movies in an air-conditioned theater - great for a relaxing break from the beach.

  • Helicopter Volcano Tours (Family Friendly - with supervision)

    • Take to the skies for a breathtaking aerial tour of Taniti's mountainous interior and active volcano.

  • Sunset Arcade & Bowling (Family Friendlly)

    • Family-friendly fun awaits with a mix of modern arcade games, a bowling alley, and casual snacks in a bright, beach-themed setting.

  • Taniti Island Art Galleries (Family Friendly)

    • Stroll through rotating exhibits of local paintings, sculpture, and crafts. Many pieces are for sale and make perfect souvenirs.
