Taniti Restaurants

  • The Driftwood Grill (Local Cuisine)

    • A laid-back beachfront restaurant known for its grilled reef fish, coconut rice, and tropical fruit desserts. Perfect for watching the sunset with a chilled drink in hand.

  • Ocean Bounty (Local Cuisine)

    • Fresh seafood straight from the harbor, prepared with traditional island spices. Favorites include fish stew and lime-marinated snapper.

  • Rainforest Table (Local Cuisine)

    • A rustic eatery tucked near the edge of the jungle, offering island-style rice bowls, taro dishes, and locally sourced produce.

  • Island Ember (Local Cuisine)

    • An upscale twist on Tanitian cuisine, offering seared tuna, spiced fish curry, and roasted plantains in a refined setting.

  • Fisherman's Catch (Local Cuisine)

    • Family-run and full of charm, this spot offers daily seafood specials based on the morning's catch, alongside house-made sauces and sides.

  • Coral Cafe (American-style)

    • A cozy diner with all-day breakfast, burgers, sandwiches, and great coffee. Popular with both locals and tourists.

  • Bayview Bistro (American-style)

    • Upscale casual dining with a mix of American favorites, from steaks and ribs to seasonal salads and pasta dishes.

  • Tropic Treats Diner (American-style)

    • Classic American comfort food served with island flair. Known for its loaded fries, milkshakes, and fried chicken.

  • Lotus Leaf (Pan-Asian Cuisine)

    • Elegant and modern, this restaurant features sushi, Thai curries, and Vietnamese-inspired noodle bowls with an island twist.

  • Bamboo Wok (Pan-Asian Cusine)

    • Casual and flavorful, Bamboo Wok offers quick stir-fry dishes, bao buns, and rich ramen with locally sourced ingredients.

Grocery Stores

  • Island Market Superstore (Supermarket)

    • Taniti's largest supermarket with a full selection of groceries, fresh produce, bakery items, and household goods.

  • Oceanview Grocers (Supermarket)

    • Modern and well-stocked, this store features international brands, local specialties, and a wide selection of seafood and meats.

  • Tropical Pantry (Small Grocery)

    • A friendly neighborhood store offering fresh produce, dairy, canned goods, and everyday essenials.

  • Bayfront Goods (Small Grocery)

    • Known for its local products and quick service, this small grocery offers fresh fruit, baked goods, and picnic-ready items.

  • QuickMart 24/7 (Convenience Store)

    • Open around the clock, QuickMart is your go-to for snacks, drinks, toiletries, and travel essentials - day or night.
