Buses and Other Ground Transportation:

  • Public Bus Service

    • Taniti City is served by a reliable public bus system that operates daily from 5:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. These buses are an affordable way to explore the capital and connect to major attractions.

  • Private Island Buses

    • For those venturing outside the city, private bus companies offer transportation to other parts of the island. Schedules and stops vary, so it's a good idea to check with your hotel or location information centers for details.

  • Taxis

    • Taxis are readily available within Taniti City and can be flagged down or booked by phone. While they are not metered, fares aretypically fixed for common routes - be sure to confirm the price before starting your trip.

  • Rental Cars

    • If you'd like to explore at your own pace, rental cars are available near the airport through a trusted local agency. Keep in mind that traffic can be busy in the city, and some rural roads may be unpaved.

  • Bicycle Rentals

    • Bicycles and helmets are available from multiple vendors throughout Taniti City and Merriton Landing. Helmets are required by law, and most rental packages include them.

  • Walking-Friendly Areas

    • Taniti City is compact and mostly flat, making it perfect for walking. The popular Merriton Landing area is also highly walkable, with pedestrian-friendly paths, local shops, resaurants, and entertainment options just steps away.

Arriving By Air or Sea

  • Taniti Island Airport

    • Most visitors arrive via the island's small but efficient airport, located just outside Taniti City. It currently accommodates small jets and proppeller planes. Expansion is underway to allow for larger commercial jets in the near future, which will further enhance accessibility.

  • Cruise Ship Docking

    • A small cruise ship makes a weekly stop in Yellow Leaf Bay, docking overnight. This provides a brief opportunity for cruise passengers to explore the island's natural beauty, beaches, and local culture.

  • Airport to City Transfers

    • Upon arrival, visitors can access taxis, rental cars, or buses to reach their lodging or tour desintations. The airport is a short drive from most major tourist areas, including Merriton Landing and Taniti City Center.
