Welcome to Taniti Island!

About the Island

Taniti is a small tropical island located in the Pacific Ocean, covering less than 500 square miles. Despite its modest size, the island features a diverse landscape that includes sandy and rocky beaches. It also boasts a safe, compact harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti is home to an indigenous population of approximately 20,000 people. Until recently, the local economy relied primarily on fishing and agriculture, but a rise in tourism has begun to reshape its economic landscape.


FAQ

  • What voltage are the power outlets?
    • 120 volts (the standard voltage of the United States)
  • What is the drinking age?
    • Officially, it is 18, although this is not strictly enforced.
  • Are there other limitations on alcohol?
    • Yes, alcohol may not by sold or served between the hours of midnight and 9:00 AM.
  • Do the local people speak English?
    • Yes, many of the younger residents speak fluent English, although residents in rural settings often will not, especially older residents.
  • Are there medical service on the island?
    • Yes, Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. These are staffed with many multilingual employees.
  • Should I worry about crime while visiting the island?
    • We encourage tourists to always be aware of their surroundings and belongings, but violent crime is very rare. We have had scattered reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.
  • Will certain shops and attractions I want to visit be open during my visit?
    • Taniti celebrates numerous national holidays during which shops and other attractions may be closed and tours may not be available. Check this site for upcoming events or contact the shop or attraction directly to inquire.
  • What currency is accepted on the island?
    • The US dollar is our official currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. We have several banks that can exchange your currency, and most businesses also accept major credit cards.
